  • Think “Out of The Box”

    The term “oversized” is commonly used for anything that cannot be shipped by use of standard containers which is a usual way in terms of shipping variety of goods and commodities. Containers come in certain sizes with limited volume and weight capacity. What you have with a standard container is basically a box that is made of steel. When we speak of oversized cargo on the other hand, it is basically something that will not fit in that box, and for that reason, some other arrangements will have to be made. We, at Projects Division, like to say think “out of the box” for planning those complex logistics arrangements.

  • Industries

    We work with our clients to increase efficiency in their logistics arrangements thus aiming at reducing costs.
    Ultra Logistics Projects has been a strong partner to many organizations operating in various industries including chemical, construction, mining and power generation.

  • Trust the cool guys

    Our dedicated sales and operations team of specialists is trained to handle your sensitive cargo, and provide seamless cold chain transportation for both frozen and chilled commodities... Whatever the commodity, we can provide a temperature-controlled environment for the safe transportation of your cargo.

  • Industry knowledge

    Our partnership with all major shipping lines allows us to offer the best transit services that comply with your products’ shelf life and cold treatment requirements. We offer a complete package of services, including but not limited to, cold storage facilities, customs clearance, FDA, Phytosanitary, Veterinary inspection and clearance, cross-docking, and trucking solutions.

  • Customer satisfaction

    Our ultimate goal is for your cargo to pass all quality controls upon arrival at its final destination, and to always ensure equipment and space availability to our valued customers. Ultra Logistics offers a full menu of logistics services to help you manage all aspects of your supply chain. We are more than just a reliable NVOCC (Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier); we act as trusted partners with our clients offering strategic advice and comprehensive services that optimize shipment operations.
